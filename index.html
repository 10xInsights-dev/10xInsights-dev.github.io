About 10xInsights

There's a 10x dev that shapes our ends; rough-hew them how we will. (Apologies to Shakespeare)

Welcome to the intersection of technology and transformation. 10xInsights is a call to arms for all aspiring to master software development and dramatically enhance their impact.

From developers to IT professionals, anyone creating software or hardware is invited. The only constant is change; what separates the good from the great is insight and adaptability.

We delve deep into insights that help top developers thrive amidst technological evolution, unraveling the mysteries of those who excel.

What we offer:

Each episode features anecdotes, experiences, and diverse perspectives from industry veterans and rising stars. We inspire and provide practical wisdom—the 'secret sauce’ for a 10x developer. This is about leading, not just keeping up.

Who should listen?

Whether you're an aspiring developer, a seasoned pro, or just curious about coding, there’s something for everyone. If you wonder what makes individuals excel or want to elevate your team's skills, 10xInsights has answers.

We believe in collaboration and sharing. Your stories, struggles, and triumphs make this initiative resonate.

How can I contribute?

Your voice matters! Join us as we explore the art of technology through our podcast, newsletter, and more. Share your experiences, nominate colleagues, and let's uncover what it means to be a 10x developer.
